# Business Rules for UK and India Markets

## 1. General Business Rules

### UK Market
- **Compliance with UK laws:** Adhere to all relevant UK regulations in e-commerce, data protection, and consumer rights.
- **Marketing regulations:** Follow the CAP code for advertising standards, ensuring all promotions are truthful and not misleading.

### India Market
- **Compliance with Indian laws:** Ensure adherence to the Consumer Protection Act, GST regulations, and e-commerce rules.
- **Local languages:** Provide support and communication in local languages where possible.

## 2. Refund Policies
- **UK:** Customers can request a full refund within 14 days of receiving the product without providing any reason. Items must be returned in original condition.
- **India:** Refund requests must be made within 30 days of delivery, contingent on the product being unused and in its original packaging, subject to local consumer laws.

## 3. Shipping Timelines
- **UK:** Standard shipping time is 3-5 working days. Express shipping is available within 1-2 working days.
- **India:** Average delivery time is 5-7 working days, with express options available for select cities at 2-3 days.

## 4. Escalation Conditions
- **Customer complaints:** Escalate complaints that remain unresolved after 48 hours to the Customer Service Manager.
- **Refund disputes:** Escalate unresolved refund requests to the Compliance and Legal team.

## 5. Kill Switch Rules
- **Usage:** The kill switch can be activated when a critical failure in the e-commerce platform is detected, requiring immediate suspension of all transactions.
- **Activation conditions:** The kill switch must be activated by senior management upon unanimous agreement after assessing the situation.

## 6. AI Guardrails
- **Data usage:** Ensure customer data is only used for optimizing service and personalization according to GDPR and Indian data protection laws.
- **Ethical AI:** All AI algorithms used must follow ethical guidelines to prevent bias and ensure fairness in customer interactions.

## 7. Cost Management
- **Budget monitoring:** Review budgets quarterly with a focus on marketing, operational, and technology expenditures.
- **Cost-cutting measures:** Implement measures if expenses exceed budgets by more than 10%.

## 8. Compliance Requirements
- **GDPR Compliance (UK):** Ensure all customer data is processed in compliance with GDPR laws.
- **Data localization (India):** Comply with local data protection regulations requiring data to be stored within India.

## Review and Update
- These business rules should be reviewed quarterly and updated as necessary to ensure compliance and relevance to market conditions.
